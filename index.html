About us

We are a group of people with similar interests, regularly meeting and discussing various topics. We have bi-weekly meetups with topics for discussion. Our past topics included global catastropic risks, cognitive biases, effective altruism and axiomatic ethics.

We have a reading group with regular meetings and we study the mathematics of artificial intelligence safety. For more info about our next meetup, see our Facebook group:

MIRI x Prague Workshops

We host workshops, where we read scientific papers about artificial intelligence safety published by the Machine Intelligence Research Institute. If you're interested, please contact Jiří Nádvorník. He wants to meet you :o).
