Community of people interested in rationality, safety of artificial intelligence, cognitive sciences, effective altruism, hpmor, self-improvement...

About Us

We are group of peole with similar interests, regularly meeting and discussing various topics. At the core are regular (every 2 weeks) meetups. Each with some prepared topic. In the past topics were things like global catastropic risks, cognitive biases, effective altruism, axiomatic ethics, and various other. Beside that there are regular meetings in reading group, and group studying mathematics of safety of artificial intelligece (bellow). For more info about next meetup see our facebook group.
